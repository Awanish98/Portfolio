Scraping The website Foreignfortune.com

It scrapes product title, description, price (per kilo and item), weight, and images. Output is a structured JSON object. Technologies: Python, Pyppeteer (headless browsing), Parsel (HTML/XML parsing), and Asyncio (asynchronous operations)."
